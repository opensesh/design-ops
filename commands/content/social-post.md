# /social-post

Guided workflow for creating social content tailored to LinkedIn, Instagram, or Substack.

## Trigger

User invokes `/social-post` when they want to create social media content with platform-specific optimization.

---

## Workflow

### Step 1: Platform Selection

"Which platform is this for?"
- **LinkedIn** - Professional audience, thought leadership
- **Instagram** - Visual-first, casual tone
- **Substack** - Long-form, newsletter subscribers

### Step 2: Post Type

"What type of post are you creating?"

**LinkedIn options:**
- Thought leadership (opinion/insight)
- Project showcase (case study snippet)
- Behind-the-scenes (process/culture)
- Educational (how-to/tips)
- Milestone/announcement

**Instagram options:**
- Carousel (educational/storytelling)
- Single image (moment/showcase)
- Reel concept (short video idea)
- Story series (ephemeral content)

**Substack options:**
- Essay (deep dive)
- Update (news/progress)
- Curation (links/resources)
- Personal (reflection/story)

### Step 3: Purpose & Goal

"What's the goal of this post?"
- Build authority
- Drive engagement
- Announce something
- Start a conversation
- Educate the audience
- Show personality

### Step 4: Content Input

"What content do you want to share? This could be:"
- An idea or insight
- A project you completed
- Something you learned
- An announcement
- Existing content to repurpose

### Step 5: Generate Draft

Based on inputs, generate platform-optimized content:

---

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn

**Format:**
```
[Hook - first line that appears before "see more"]

[Body - 3-5 short paragraphs]

[CTA or question]

[Hashtags - 3-5 relevant]
```

**Best practices:**
- First line is everything (hook before the fold)
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- Use line breaks liberally
- Personal stories outperform generic advice
- Questions drive comments
- Post length: 150-300 words optimal

### Instagram

**Caption format:**
```
[Hook - first 125 characters crucial]

[Body - conversational, emoji-friendly]

[CTA]

.
.
.

[Hashtags - up to 30, but 5-10 quality ones better]
```

**Best practices:**
- Hook must work in preview (125 chars)
- Carousel posts get more saves
- Mix of broad and niche hashtags
- Include alt text recommendations
- CTA: save, share, comment, link in bio

### Substack

**Format:**
```
[Strong headline]

[Opening hook - first 2 sentences crucial for preview]

[Body - structured with headers]

[Closing + CTA]
```

**Best practices:**
- Headline is 80% of the battle
- First paragraph in email preview - make it count
- Use subheadings for scanability
- End with a question or CTA to encourage replies
- Link to previous posts when relevant

## Step 6: Image/Visual Guidance

After generating copy, offer:

"For the visual, consider:"
- [Specific recommendations based on content]
- [Style suggestions aligned with brand]
- [Alt text recommendation for accessibility]

## Step 7: Review & Refine

After presenting the draft:

- "Want me to adjust the tone? (more casual/professional)"
- "Should I create variations for A/B testing?"
- "Need a shorter/longer version?"
- "Want me to generate this for another platform too?"
